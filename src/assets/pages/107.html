We use superlative adjectives to compare one thing with a group of things.
We add
● –est to short adjectives like fast.
● We put most in front of long adjectives of two syllables or more, like famous.
● We put the in front of superlative adjectives.
Some adjectives like
● good and bad have irregular superlative forms: the best and the worst.
