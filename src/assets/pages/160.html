A9.1 Introduction: Traditional Ethiopian culture
Ethiopia has a rich tradition of both secular and religious music, singing and dancing,
and these together constitute an important part of Ethiopian cultural life. Singing
accompanies many agricultural activities, as well as religious festivals and ceremonies
surrounding life’s milestones, such as birth, marriage and death.
1 What cultural activities do you know of or take part in?
2 Work in groups to make notes of different festivals that involve traditional dancing, and
make notes about them.
