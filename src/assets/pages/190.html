B10.2 Reading: Newspaper reports
How often do you read a newspaper? It is a good idea to look at one as often as you can. It will
increase your general knowledge and help your reading skills. You don’t need to read the whole
paper, just the parts that interest you. Reading the headline can help you to decide if the article is
going to be interesting or not.
