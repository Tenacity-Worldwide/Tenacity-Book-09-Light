B3.1 Speaking: Agreeing and disagreeing
1 You are going to have a class discussion on this subject: Listening to the radio is a waste of time.
1 Work in a group with people who have the same opinion as you. In your group do the following:
● Think of points to support your opinion. For example: Listening to the radio is important because
without it we would not learn about world events.
● Think about possible arguments against your viewpoint and what you can say against them. For
example, Argument 1: You can waste a lot of time listening to pop music.
Argument 2: Yes, but it gives me pleasure to hear local Ethiopian artists on the radio.
