B11.4 Writing: An endangered animal
1 Find out all the information you can about an endangered animal, for example, a gorilla, a
rhino or an elephant. Use an encyclopaedia or the Internet to check your facts.
2 Pretend that you are that animal and write a story called A day in the life of an animal. Try
to make the story interesting by using many adjectives and different tenses.
