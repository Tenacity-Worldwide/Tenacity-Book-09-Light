Work in a small group and discuss your
answers to these questions.

Ethiopia and in your local region?

stations in Ethiopia?

a read a newspaper
b listen to the radio
c watch TV
4 When do you do each of these things?
5 What kind of articles do you like reading in the
newspaper?
6 What kind of programmes do you like listening
to on the radio?
7 What kind of programmes do you like watching
on TV?
