B5.10 Fun with words: How many words can you make?
How many words can you make from this word? characteristics
In each word you can use each letter as many times as it appears in the word:
●
For example. c x 3, h x 1
Words must have two or more letters
●
Try to make at least 12 words.
●

order. Put them in the right order, then tell your friend the story.

Your teacher will give you a short extract from The African Child; it is the autobiography of Camara

questions about the extract. Read the text and answer the questions in your exercise book.
