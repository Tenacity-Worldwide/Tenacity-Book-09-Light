Doctor: Good morning, please sit down. How (1) _____ I help you?
Patient: It’s my arm, doctor. It’s very painful.
Doctor: Let me have a look. Oh dear! That does not look very good. What happened?
Patient: Well, I cut it on a nail that was sticking out of a wall. That was about a week ago.
I thought it was getting better, but as you (2) _____ see, it isn’t.
Doctor: No, it’s very swollen now. It’s infected. Did you wash it when you cut it?
Patient: No, doctor, I didn’t think it was very bad.
Doctor: Now you see what happens when you just leave it! When you cut yourself you (3) _____
wash it and cover it. I’m going to give you some tablets. You (4) _____ take one three

Patient: Yes, doctor.
Doctor: The nurse will dress this wound. You (6) _____ come back in two days so that she

Patient: No, doctor. Do I (9) _____ stay at home?
Doctor: No, you (10) _____ go to school, but you (11) _____ be careful. You (12) _____ run
