B9.10 Fun with words: A preposition game
Write in, on and at on separate sheets of paper. When your teacher gives you a time
phrase, choose the correct preposition and hold it up. Students who choose the wrong
preposition are out of the game.
Example:
Teacher: the weekend Students hold up the word at to make at the weekend.
B9.11 Study skills: Vocabulary strategies

you meet your target? Explain to your partner what strategies you used and whether they
were successful.
